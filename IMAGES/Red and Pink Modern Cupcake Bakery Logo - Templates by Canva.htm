Bakery logos by Canva

Whip up a fresh brand image for your bakery business and entice prospective customers with Canva’s delectable bakery logo templates. Whether you’re a master pastry chef or a budding baker, you can create a logo that perfectly captures the sweetness of your work.

The right logo design for your bakery shop is the secret ingredient to success. With Canva’s generous selection of bakery logo ideas, you can cook up virtually any style, theme, or motif you like for your brand and your target audience. Choose from hundreds of novel and attractive designs featuring cute cupcakes and pastries, adorable little chefs, charming kitchen tools, and more. These templates blend style and simplicity, making them the perfect premade canvas for your unique creation.

Craft something sweet and simple with our minimalist bakery logo design templates and make a memorable logo that goes well on anything, be it the sign on your shopfront, the thumbnail for your website, or the baggies that customers take home. Or grab one of our vibrant and colorful template options and get creative with a one-of-a-kind design to set yourself apart from the competition.

Whether you’re opening a cozy neighborhood bakery or setting up a website to promote your existing business, Canva makes it easy to make any kind of bakery logo design. Personalize your chosen logo template to match your bakery’s branding and aesthetic. Adjust the colors, fonts, and design elements. Add your business name and tagline. Save your logo as a high-resolution, background-free image to use for other collaterals, like your social media posts or packaging materials.

When you’re ready, share your work with the world. Download a digital copy of your new bakery logo design to use on your website and social media pages. Save your design in your Canva account so you can use it on your branded materials like your business cards, signages, packaging wrappers, and other collaterals.

With Canva, making delectable, professional designs is as easy as pie. Customize and download our free bakery logo templates today.
